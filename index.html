Our Mission

Empowering Sudanese Youth Through Robotics

First Global Team Sudan is dedicated to fostering innovation, technical skills, and international collaboration among Sudanese youth through robotics competitions and STEM education.

Innovation

We foster creative problem-solving and innovative thinking to address global challenges through technology.

Collaboration

We build bridges across cultures, promoting international cooperation and teamwork.

Education

We provide hands-on learning experiences in STEM fields to prepare youth for future careers.

About Us

Our Journey in Global Robotics

First Global Team Sudan was established in 2017 to represent Sudan in international robotics competitions. Our team brings together talented students from across the country who are passionate about technology and innovation.

Through mentorship, training, and participation in global competitions, we aim to develop the next generation of Sudanese engineers, scientists, and technology leaders who will contribute to Sudan's technological advancement.
